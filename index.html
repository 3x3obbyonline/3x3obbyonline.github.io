About 3x3 Obby

3x3 Obby is a wildly popular obstacle course platformer that challenges players to navigate through a series of increasingly difficult levels. Unlike traditional parkour games, 3x3 Obby incorporates unique grid mechanics where precision jumping is key. Whether you are a speedrunner looking to set a new record or a casual gamer wanting to pass the time, this game offers endless replayability.

Level Strategies

The Starter Plains

Focus on basic jumps. The gaps between the 3x3 platforms are standard width. Don't rush; timing is forgiving here.

The Rotating Gears

Wait for the gears to align with the grid. Jump slightly earlier than you think to account for rotation speed.

Lava Grid

Red platforms are fake! Memorize the path shown at the start of the level or follow the slightly darker tiles.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?
Progress is saved automatically in your browser's local storage. As long as you don't clear your cache, you can return to the last checkpoint you reached.
Is 3x3 Obby difficult?
It starts easy! The first world introduces you to the mechanics. However, later levels (World 2 and 3) require precise timing and quick reflexes, making it a fun challenge for skilled players.
Why is the screen black?
If the game doesn't load, check your internet connection or try disabling ad blockers, as they sometimes interfere with HTML5 game scripts. Refreshing the page usually solves the issue.
